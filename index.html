Estructura inf verb + s/es (he, she, it)
 (I) am / (he, she, it) is / (you, we, they) are + verb-ing
Forma larga: am / is / are
Forma corta: ‘m / ‘re / ‘s		 verb past tense (regular verb + ed or irregular verb)
 (I, he, she, it) was / (you, we, they) were + verb-ing
 have/has + past participle
Forma larga: have / has
Forma corta: 've / 's		 had + past participle
Forma larga: had
Forma corta: 'd		 will + inf verb
Forma larga: will
Forma corta: 'll'
Ejemplo (afirmativa) She writes the report.
She is writing the report.
She wrote the report.
She was writing the report.
She has finished the report.
She had finished the report before the meeting.
She will write the report.
Ejemplo (negativa) They do not send the email.
Forma larga: do not
Forma corta: They don't send the email. 		They are not sending the email.
Forma larga: are not
Forma corta: They aren't sending the email. 		They did not send the email.
Forma larga: did not
Forma corta: They didn't send the email. 		They were not sending the email.
Forma larga: were not
Forma corta: They weren't sending the email. 		They have not sent the email.
Forma larga: have not
Forma corta: They haven't sent the email. 		They had not sent the email.
Forma larga: had not
Forma corta: They hadn't sent the email. 		They will not sent the email.
Forma larga: will not
Forma corta: They won't sent the email.
Ejemplo (interrogativa) Does he check the server?
Is he checking the server?
 Did he check the server?
 Was he checking the server?
 Have you checked the server?
Had you checked the server?
Will you check the server?
